About Us

Morehouse Pizza Kitchen was opened in 2025, located in Kilgore Hall. Since then, we have made it our mission to make the best pizza possible for Men of Morehouse. We understand and respect Morehouse's culture, so it is a true honor to serve the world's next Morehouse Men.
